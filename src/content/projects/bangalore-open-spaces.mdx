## Project Overview

This study, conducted in 2016, focused on the planning and policy aspects of open spaces in Bangalore. It aimed to understand the usage patterns and cultural significance of these spaces, as well as evaluate their provision across different wards in the city.

The study explored various aspects of open space management, including operations and maintenance, and investigated strategies to enhance public engagement with these spaces at both the city and neighborhood levels. The research sought to provide insights and recommendations for improving the quality and accessibility of open spaces in Bangalore, contributing to the city's overall livability and sustainability.
